Steve Reich Is Playing Zelda

What is this?
Zelda Phase

Piano Phase is minimalist composition by American composer Steve Reich, written in 1967 for two pianos. The two pianos have identical lines of music, which begin by playing synchronously, but slowly become out of phase with one another when one of them slightly speeds up.

The Legend of Zelda is a most excellent game. The sound being looped on this page is the fragment that plays when a puzzle in the game is solved.

Craig M. Booth, 2017
