NFL Statsalyzer

Interactive NFL statistics exploration

Select from bubble plots, bar charts and line graphs using the tabs. Choose what to plot by clicking axis labels, select colors and point sizes to the right. Animate through time with the play button.

 

 

What is this?

The Statsalyzer is a data mining and visualization project that marries data from Armchair Analysis to the Google charts API to allow an interactive exploration of how every NFL team performed since the year 2000. The stats that have been calculated for the teams are:

 

1st Quarter Points: Total points scored by this team in the first quarter this year

2nd Quarter Points: Total points scored by this team in the second quarter this year

3rd Quarter Points: Total points scored by this team in the third quarter this year

4th Quarter Points: Total points scored by this team in the fourth quarter this year

Defensive Points: Total points scored by the defense this year

Fumbles Lost: Total number of fumbles lost by the team in this year

Interceptions Made: Total number of interceptions by the team's defense this year

Losses: Total number of losses this year

Passing First Downs: Total number of first downs gained by passing this year

Passing Yards: Total offensive yards gained by passing in this year

Penalty Yards (Against): Total pentaly yards assessed against team this year

Points Conceded: Total points conceded this year

Points Scored: Total points scored this year

Punts: Total number of punts made by the team this year

QB Rushing Attempts: Total rushing attempts by the QB this year

QB Rushing Yards: Total rushing yards by QB this year

Rushing First Downs: Total number of first downs gained by rushing this year

Rushing Yards: Total offensive rushing yards this year

Sacks Given Up: Total number of times the team's QB was sacked this year

Wins: Total number of wins by the team this year
